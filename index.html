The history of the human species goes as far back as 300,000 years. There have been many changes in our history that have led the human species to where it is now. Three significant revolutions that changed human society and behavior. Throughout this journey we weren't alone. We had other species of humans that roamed the Earth before us and alongside us. There is still much speculation as to what led to the extinction of our counterparts. Through the study of these other human species, we can uncover the secrets of our past.
