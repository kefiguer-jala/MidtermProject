Different Human Species

Homo neanderthalensis

  • About 400,000 - 40,000 years ago
  • Nickname: Neanderthal
  • Discovery Date: 1829
  • Where Lived: Europe and southwestern to central Asia

Homo rudolfensis

  • About 1.9 million to 1.8 million years ago
  • Discovery Date: 1986
  • Where Lived: Eastern Africa (northern Kenya, possibly northern Tanzania and Malawi)

Homo sapiens

  • About 300,000 years ago to present
  • Where Lived: Evolved in Africa, now worldwide
